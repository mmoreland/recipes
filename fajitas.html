Grilled Chicken Fajitas

Grilled Fajitas are a great summertime meal no matter how busy you are, simply marinate, and let it sit until you are ready to cook
This recipe can be broken up into two parts, the chicken and the veggies, they can be done seperatly or at the same time

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • Bottle of lime juice
  • Gralic Salt
  • Tortillas
  • 2 Bell Peppers
  • 1 large yellow Onion
  • Olive Oil
  • Tin Foil
  • Salsa of choice
  • Guacamole
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Sour Cream

Instructions

Chicken

  1. Take your chicken breasts and cut it up into strips
  2. Season generously with Garlic Salt
  3. Place strips into ziploc and fill with Lime juice until chicken is fully submerged
  4. Let chicken sit in fridge for at least 45 min. The longer the better
  5. Once the chicken is marinated, place onto the grill on high heat
  6. Let them cook until there is some crisp on the chicken, varies by strip size. Ususally about 8 min a side

Veggies

  1. Preheat 1 tbsp of Olive Oil on a cast iron skillet to a medium or medium/low heat
  2. Cut Onion and Bell peppers into strips
  3. Place veggies into preheated skillet, there should be just a little of that sizzle sound
  4. Let the veggies sit on the heat for a couple minutes to get some color
  5. After about 3-4 min, lower the heat just a bit, and toss veggies in skillet so that everything is covered the oil
  6. Let that carmelize, until desired color, usually 30 min. Shift veggies around in the skillet ocasionally.

Put it together

  1. Preheat your oven to 200
  2. Wrap your tortillas in tin foil and place in oven
  3. Once those are warm take out of oven
  4. Put it all together by placing some chicken, veggies, and toppings of choice on Fajita
