Grilled Chicken Fajitas

Grilled Fajitas are a great summertime meal no matter how busy you are, simply marinate, and let it sit until you are ready to cook

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • Bottle of lime juice
  • Gralic Salt
  • Tortillas
  • 2 Bell Peppers
  • 1 large yellow Onion
  • Olive Oil
  • Tin Foil
