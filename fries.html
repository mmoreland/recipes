Recipe for Air Fryer Fries

"Air fryer french fries can be a great healthy alternative to normal French Fries , as it reduces the calories that come from a batter and oil"

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Russet Potato
  • Spray on Olive Oil
  • Garlic Salt
  • Paprika
  • Black Pepper or Lemon Pepper

Directions

  1. Cut up your Russet Potato into fries, I usually cut it in half and then cut up each half
  2. Place cut up potato in a bowl fill with water
  3. Let potatos soak in water for about 30 minutes to remove some starchiness
  4. Once, soaked, take out of water and pat dry
  5. Spary a light amount of olive oil on fries and toss to evenly spread
  6. Season to taste; and seasoning can be used from here, the ones listed are a pretty standard recipe to the ratio below
  7. 2 Paprika: 1 Garlic Salt: 1/2 Pepper
  8. Place in Air fryer and cook for 20 min at 375 or until a golden brown color
