Baked Ziti with Italian Sausage

Baked Ziti is a great recipe for an easy sunday night, I prefer to get spicy italian suasage with mine but the recipe works with non-spicy or even some ground beef with extra seasoning if you are trying to get something leaner.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of Ziti or Rigatoni
  • 1 lb of Italian sausage
  • 1 package of Marinara Sauce
  • 1 bag of Shredded Mozzerella
  • 1/2 cup of shredded Parmesean
  • Pinch of Sea Salt
  • Tinfoil

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350*
  2. Fill a pot with water and heat to a boil
  3. Once water is boiling, season with Sea Salt, and place pasta in
  4. Take Pasta out once Al Dente(check box for cook time)
  5. Brown your Italian suasage on a pan on Medium high heat.
  6. In a cooking tray, place Pasta, Sausage, and Marinara
  7. Mix around until even distribution, top with 1/2 cup Mozz, and Parm each
  8. Cover in Tin Foil and bake for 30 min
  9. Once 30 min are up, take out of oven, take off tin foil, top with a bit more cheese
  10. Enjoy
